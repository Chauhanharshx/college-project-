Admissions

Begin your journey with a clear, student-focused admissions process.

How to Apply

  1. Review program details and admission requirements.
  2. Complete the online application form with transcripts and supporting documents.
  3. Submit your statement of purpose and recommendation letters.
  4. Receive your admission decision and confirm your seat.

Key Dates

  • Spring Intake: January 10
  • Summer Intake: May 15
  • Fall Intake: August 20

Need help? Our admissions team is available Monday through Friday for personalized guidance.
